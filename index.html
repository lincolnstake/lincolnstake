Lincoln Stake, Idaho :: Photo and Video Submission

In order to encourage participation in Stake activities, we are accepting recent pictures from church activities that we can show during stake conference.

Since we are volunteers, and there is the potential for a wide range of submissions, we ask that you consider the following:

Requests:

  • We combine the collected photos every six months for conference so photos you submit should be no older than roughly six months old.
  • Images should be identified so that we know what they are from. For ward activities, include the Ward name. Date, Location, and Event/Activity are useful. You could put this information in a folder name, in a text file in the applicable folder, or in the individual file name. (*See note about missionaries)
  • Do not worry about labeling people in the photo at this time. (*See note about missionaries)
  • Be sure to have permission from the person that snapped the photo
  • Try to submit only tasteful photos with which the parents of or people in the photos would be okay
  • You do not need to send a large quantity of photos/video. Quality is probably better than quantity here

Please Do Not Submit

  • Photos that include people who do not represent the teachings of the Church in their dress, grooming, gestures, and so on.
  • Out-of-focus or blurry photos
  • Photos of people in swimming suits

How to Submit

  • Media may be submitted to either your ward (or branch) historian or directly to this dropbox. This is faster than submitting them by email.
  • Dropbox will ask for your name and email address. This will allow us to contact you if we have further questions.
  • You may combine all of your media into a zip file if that is easier for you.

*Note About Missionaries

Is there a full-time missionary in your family? We want to show a little bit of what missionaries from our stake are doing. Send up to 3 photos or a video and we will consider showing them during Stake Conference. These don't have to be serious but they should be church-appropriate. Missionaries will be identified by name; please include their name as part of the filename.

Thank you for your efforts!
