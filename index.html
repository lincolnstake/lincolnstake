Lincoln Stake Photo Submission Procedure

In order to encourage participation in activities within the stake, we are accepting recent pictures from church activities that we can show during stake conference.

 

Please consider the following during your submissions:

 

Requests

•         Since we combine the collected photos every six months for conference, photos should portray activities from the last six months.

•         Images should be identified so that we know what they are from.

o   For ward activities, include the ward name.

o   Noting the date, location, event and, activity is useful.

o   You could put this information in a folder name, in a text file in the applicable folder, or in the individual file name.

o   Do not worry about labeling people in the photo unless your historian needs that. (*See note about missionaries.)

•         Be sure to have permission from the person that snapped the photo.

•         Aim to submit tasteful photos that are not embarrassing.

•         You don’t need to send a large quantity of photos/video. Quality is probably better than quantity here.

 

Please Do Not Submit

•         Photos that include people who do not represent the teachings of the Church in their dress, grooming, gestures, and so on.

•         Out-of-focus or blurry photos.

•         Photos of people in swimming suits.

 

How to Submit

•         Media may be submitted to either your ward (or branch) historian or directly to this dropbox. This is faster than submitting them by email.

•         Dropbox will ask for your name and email address. This will allow us to contact you if we have further questions.

•         You may combine all your media into a zip file if that is easier for you.

 

Note About Missionaries

Is there a full-time missionary in your family? We want to show a little bit of what missionaries from our stake are doing. Send up to 3 photos or a video and we will consider showing them during Stake Conference. These do not have to be serious but they should be church-appropriate.

•         Missionaries will be identified by name; please include their name as part of the filename.

 

Thank you for your efforts!
